Experiment 2 - starry-summit-silence

Translated from cmpm147-ex02-starry-summit-silence on glitch.com

Description

An interactive p5.js canvas reflecting the serene hues and dynamic contours of a mountain under a celestial tapestry.

Technical

A gradient mirroring the sunset. Parallax effects for both sides. Stars are represented with simple ellipses and are made shimmering through sinusoidal adjustments, while the shooting star follows the mouse. Mountain peaks and snowcaps are generated randomly.

Reflection

Tweaking the effect of the shimmering stars and the parallel effects took a while. GPT help was requested.
