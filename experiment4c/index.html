Experiment 4c - Infinite Portals

Description

Clicking a tile opens up a new webpage. Links to places in Google Maps.

Technical

Infinite Portals utilizes a combination of JavaScript, the p5.js library, and procedural generation algorithms to render a responsive and interactive digital environment. The core mechanic involves clicking on tiles, which triggers visual changes and sounds, simulating the effect of opening portals to other dimensions.

Reflection

Getting the background music to play through p5.sound.min.js, and intergrating to portfolio.
