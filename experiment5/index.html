Description

A generator that directly reads inspiring images, renders parameterized images, and adjusts its own designs to optimize an evaluation score.

Technical

Integration of Inspiring Images: The project incorporates a selection of three inspiring images accessible via a dropdown menu, which users can choose from to influence the behavior and output of the image generator.

Initialization Based on Inspiration: The initialization function, p4_initialize, adapts its behavior depending on the selected inspiration, leading to unique starting conditions for each image, affecting how the generator interprets these images initially.

Rendering Related to Inspiration: The rendering function, p4_render, is designed to process and display results that, though abstract, maintain a visual or thematic connection to the chosen inspiring image, especially after employing a 30-second annealing technique to refine the output.

Adaptive Mutation Process: The mutation function, p4_mutate, introduces gradual and controlled changes to the design, with a user-controllable mutation rate slider that allows fine-tuning of the exploration intensity, ranging from near-static to highly dynamic transformations.
